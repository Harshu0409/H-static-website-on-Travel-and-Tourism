Why Visit New York City?

New York is a popular tourist destination with many attractions, including museums, historic sites, and other landmarks:
  • Museums: The Metropolitan Museum of Art is one of the most visited museums in the world. The Intrepid Sea-Air-Space Museum is housed on a World War II aircraft carrier.

  • Historic sites: Ellis Island and the Statue of Liberty are popular immigration sites.

  • Landmarks: The Empire State Building was the world's tallest building for 41 years after it was constructed in 1931. Central Park is considered one of the world's finest examples of landscape architecture.

  • Other attractions: Times Square, Broadway shows, Radio City Music Hall, and Wall Street are other popular attractions.

  • Free attractions: Central Park, Times Square, and the New York Public Library are free to visit. New York City is known for its energy and vibrancy, with its neon lights, skyscrapers, and nightlife. It's also a cosmopolitan city with ethnic enclaves, shops, and brownstones.
